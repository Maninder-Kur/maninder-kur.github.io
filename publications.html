TinyH2GNet: Exploring Lightweight Models for Gene Expression Prediction from H&E Images

Authors: Maninder Kaur, Amit Kumar, Ankita Rani, Raghvendra Mall, Puneet Goyal, Sukrit Gupta

28th International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention (MICCAI 2026)
Accepted for MICCAI 2026

Introduces TinyH2GNet, a study investigating whether lightweight architectures can achieve competitive gene expression prediction from H&E histopathology images. Through extensive benchmarking of CNN-, Transformer-, and Graph-based models across multiple spatial transcriptomics datasets, the work demonstrates that substantially smaller models can retain predictive performance while reducing computational cost, FLOPs, and inference time, highlighting a strong accuracy-efficiency trade-off for image-to-gene prediction.
